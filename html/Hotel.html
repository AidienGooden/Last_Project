Hotel recommendations

Top

Hotel LeVeque, Autograph Collection

An experience at Hotel LeVeque is a dazzling escape. The 149 room boutique hotel located in the heart of Downtown Columbus is sure to enchant each guest with stunning details ranging from custom, locally curated bath amenities to an inspiring signature starry turndown service

Hilton Columbus at Easton

Perfectly situated in the heart of Easton Town Center where you will find the best shopping, dining and entertainment literally steps away.

Hampton Inn & Suites Columbus-Downtown

The Nationwide Arena and dozens of restaurants are within a 10-minute walk—or use the free Cbus to explore downtown. Our indoor pool, fitness center, and free WiFi and daily hot breakfast complete your stay.

The Blackwell

Our hotel is located close to all the best attractions in Columbus. Explore popular sites like the Columbus Zoo and Aquarium, Center of Science and Industry, and Short North during your stay.

Drury Plaza Hotel Columbus Downtown

Guests of Drury Plaza Hotel Columbus Downtown have easy access to the Greater Columbus Convention Center through an adjoining walkway. The hotel is close to other downtown attractions like the Short North Arts District and The Ohio State University. Shopping at North Market or catching a game in the Arena district is just a short walk away.

Hampton Inn & Suites Columbus/University Area

Enjoy free hot breakfast, free WiFi, and our indoor pool.

Hilton Columbus Downtown

Nationwide Arena and North Market are two blocks away. Enjoy our art collection, with pieces by Central Ohio artists. We offer an indoor saline pool, whirlpool, and two fitness centers, one featuring a Peloton® bike with toe cages.

Le Méridien Columbus, The Joseph

Welcome to your newest adventure at Le Méridien Columbus, The Joseph. Within a mile of our spa hotel, reach storied attractions such as Italian Village, North Market, Greater Columbus Convention Center, Nationwide Arena and the downtown core.

The Urban

Now introducing our short-term stay rooms at The Urban! With incredible amenities and stylish hotel rooms, you will have a tidy and fun place to call home in between your time at concerts, ball games, festivals, university events, and all that Columbus, Ohio has to offer.

Moxy Hotel Columbus/Short North

Tired of an ordinary hotel check-in experience? So are we. At Moxy Columbus Short North, you check in 24/7 where it matters - at The Bar. Don’t forget to grab your free drink token along with your room keys! Our open hotel lobby is designed to keep you coming back.
